— Movement Point Costs Reference —
🛤️
Your Own Road
1 MP — cheapest movement. Build roads strategically to create fast trade routes.
🛣️
Opponent's Road
1 MP + 1 Gold paid to road owner. Bridges cost 1 MP + 2 Gold to use.
🌿
No Road (open path)
2 MP — off-road travel is slow. Building roads reduces your own MP costs.
⚔️
Path with Barbarian
+2 extra MP on top of normal path cost. Drive off barbarians to clear the way!

🏛️ VARIANT & SPECIAL CARDS Harbormaster · Friendly Robber · 2-Player · Special Tiles
⚓
Harbormaster Card
Variant Card — worth +2 Victory Points.

Awarded to the player with the most Harbor Points (minimum 3). A settlement adjacent to a harbor = 1 Harbor Point. A city adjacent to a harbor = 2 Harbor Points.

If you lose the lead, the Harbormaster card passes to the new leader. Victory total to win increases by 1 when this variant is used.
😊
Friendly Robber
Variant Rule — no card, just a rule change.

The robber may not be placed on a hex adjacent to a player's settlements who has 2 or fewer Victory Points. Protects weaker players from being bullied.

Compatible with any scenario. Often combined with Event Cards or standard play.
🪙
Trade Token (2-Player Variant)
Used in Catan for Two (2-Player Variant).

Earned by building near desert/coastal hexes or sacrificing a Knight card. Trade tokens enable special actions: costs 2 tokens for the leading player and 1 token for the trailing player.

Token Actions include: forced trades with opponent, extra resource draws, blocking neutral player expansion.
🐪
Camel Token (Great Caravan)
Used in The Great Caravan scenario.

Camel figures mark the caravan route across the desert. Players guide their camels from oasis to oasis, earning gold and resources for each stop completed. Each camel placement must face a free intersection at the oasis hex.

First to complete the route earns a Victory Point bonus.
🌉
Bridge
Used in Traders & Barbarians scenario.

Bridges are built across rivers. Cost: 1 Ore + 1 Wood + 1 Brick. Moving your wagon across a bridge costs only 1 MP (vs 3 MP without a bridge).

Opponents can use your bridge for 1 MP + 2 Gold paid to you.
Build: ⛏ Ore🪵 Wood🧱 Brick
🏰
Castle / Victory Point Cards
Commodity Delivery VP tokens.

Each successful delivery to the correct trade hex (Castle ← Glass/Marble, Quarry ← Tools, Glassworks ← Sand) earns 1 Victory Point token AND gold based on your Baggage Train level.

13 VP needed to win in Traders & Barbarians scenario.
